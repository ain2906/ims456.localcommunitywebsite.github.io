Kampung Sri Gunung Pulai

About Us

Reference







  • History

    • Kampung Sri Gunung Pulai, was opened in 1955 by two friends, the late Mr. Mohid Bin Kassan and the late Mr. Ahmad Sufian Bin Ishak.


  • Village Name

    • Kampung Sri Gunung Pulai was taken based on its position at the foot of Gunung Pulai. In addition, there are many pulai trees around it which is the main factor in choosing the name.


  • Goal of the Village

    • Kampung Sri Gunung Pulai is a traditional village that emphasizes the development of infrastructure and human capital. The JKKK and the residents will make every effort to ensure that this village is harmonious and prosperous.


  • Vision and Objectives

    • Ensuring the community is healthy and productive, giving birth to an educated community, ensuring the village is always clean, beautiful and cheerful. Cultivating the spirit of cooperation among residents, forming human capital based on Al-Quran and Al-Hadith.
