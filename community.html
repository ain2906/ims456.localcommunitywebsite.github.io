Kampung Sri Gunung Pulai

Local Community

Reference







  • Community

    • Kampung Sri Gunung Pulai is a village located in Kulai, Johor which is located approximately 51.4 kilometers from Johor Bahru. Most of the majority of the population is from the Malays and Chinese and runs the oil palm economy, farming, and other jobs.


  • Occupation

    • The majority of the inhabitants of the Sri Gunung Pulai area work in the private sector, public servants, entrepreneurs and farmers.


  • Religion and Race

    • A total of 520 people are from the Malay race while the rest are from the Chinese race. All Malays are Muslims.
